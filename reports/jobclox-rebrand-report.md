## Remaining References

Remaining `Persevex` / `persevex` references are intentionally not changed in this brand-only pass where they are not user-facing, or where changing them could affect persistence or historical evidence.

- Persistence and contract identifiers:
  - `lib/auth/cookies.ts` keeps `persevex_session` and `persevex_role`.
  - `src/lib/sessionClient.ts`, `src/lib/theme-constants.ts`, and `src/components/CandidateDashboard.tsx` keep existing localStorage keys.
  - `services/jobService.ts`, `services/jobImportService.ts`, `lib/jobs/workflow.ts`, and `app/api/jobs/create/route.ts` retain `Persevex Internal` and `getPersevexInternalCompanyId` for the existing internal-company database lookup.
- Code/CSS identifiers and comments:
  - `.persevex-aurora`, `persevexBackgroundFlow`, `persevexProductTokens`, and related comments remain as internal implementation identifiers.
- Historical artifacts:
  - `docs/`, `docs/archive/`, `supabase/migrations/`, and `scripts/maintenance/` still contain old-brand references in reports, fixtures, smoke-test email domains, and migration comments.
- Legacy asset:
  - `public/persevex_logo.avif` remains in the repo as an unused legacy asset.
